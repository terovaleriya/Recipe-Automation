Simple simnel bites

  • Vegetarian

Ingredients

125g shelled pistachios
1 x Waitrose Rich & Juicy Fruit Cake
500g Cooks' Homebaking White Marzipan
Cornflour, for dusting
4 tbsp apricot jam  

Method

1. Place the pistachios in a food processor and blitz until they become roughly the texture of large breadcrumbs — a few bigger bits here and there isn’t a problem. Tip the pistachios onto a plate.

2. Unwrap the cake and slice it in half down the whole length. Lay the 2 long pieces in front of you and slice each one into 3 long 'fingers’. You should end up with 6 long, thin pieces of cake.

3. Divide the marzipan into 6 equal pieces. Lightly dust a clean work surface with cornflour and roll out each piece of marzipan until roughly 16 x 9cm in size and the thickness of a £1 coin. Brush the top with apricot jam and lay one of the fruit cake fingers onto the front edge. Roll up the finger, trimming any leftovers so the cake is perfectly wrapped, but left open at each end.

4. Brush the wrapped fruit cake with apricot jam then press all sides into the blitzed pistachios until it is well coated all over. Repeat with the remaining slices of cake and marzipan. Cut each finger into 8 pieces and store in an airtight container for up to 4 days.
