Spiced honeyed aubergines with harissa couscous

  • Vegetarian

Ingredients

1 tbsp olive oil
1 large aubergine, quartered lengthways then cut into bitesize chunks
125g couscous
2 tsp Cooks’ Ingredients Ruby Rose Harissa Paste
Juice ½ lemon
25g pack flat leaf parsley, chopped
25g pack mint, leaves only, chopped
2 tsp clear honey 

Method

1. Heat the oil in a large frying pan. Add the aubergine, season and cook for 10 minutes, turning regularly, until golden and charring and completely tender. A gentle squash with the wooden spoon will help any last bits to yield to the heat.

2. Meanwhile, place the couscous in a small bowl with 1 tsp harissa and pour over 150ml boiling water. Cover with clingfilm and leave
for 5 minutes then fluff up with a fork, stir in the lemon juice, herbs and seasoning.

3. When the aubergines are ready, stir in the honey and remaining harissa and take the pan off the heat. Divide the couscous between
2 plates. Spoon over the aubergines and serve with tzatziki or natural yogurt if you wish.
