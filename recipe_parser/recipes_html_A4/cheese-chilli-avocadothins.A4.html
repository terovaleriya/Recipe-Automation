Cheese, chilli & avocado thins

Vegetarian

Ingredients

2 ripe avocados
2 tbsp light mayonnaise
2 tbsp Cooksâ€™ Ingredients Hot Chilli Sauce
Pack of 6 Warburtons Soft White Sliced Sandwich Thins
150g Anchor Lighter Mature Cheddar Cheese, grated
50g watercress, thick stalks removed
 

Method

1. Mash the avocado flesh with the mayonnaise and chilli sauce then spread the thins on both sides.

2. Sprinkle the cheese over the bases and then add the watercress. Top with the lids and serve.
