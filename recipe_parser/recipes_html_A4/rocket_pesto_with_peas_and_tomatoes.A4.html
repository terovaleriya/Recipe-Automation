Rocket pesto with peas and tomatoes

  • Vegetarian

Ingredients

  • 375g essential Waitrose spaghetti
  • 250g essential Waitrose frozen garden peas
  • 100g wild rocket
  • 3 tbsp grated essential Waitrose parmigiano reggiano
  • 4 tbsp olive oil
  • Â½ lemon, grated zest and juice
  • 12 sundried tomatoes, chopped

Method

  1. Cook the spaghetti in a pan of boiling water, according to packet instructions, adding the peas to the pan 3 minutes before the end.
  2. Meanwhile, blitz together the rocket, parmigiano reggiano, olive oil and lemon zest and juice in a food processor, until finely chopped and combined.
  3. Drain the pasta, then stir through the pesto mixture and tomatoes. Serve with extra grated parmigiano reggiano if liked.
