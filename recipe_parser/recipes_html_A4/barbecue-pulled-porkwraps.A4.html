Barbecue pulled pork wraps

Ingredients

250g pack Waitrose Microwaveable Basmati Pilau Rice
180g pack Cooksâ€™ Ingredients Pulled Ham Hock
100g frozen sweetcorn, defrosted
5 tbsp barbecue sauce
4 essential Waitrose Wholemeal Tortilla Wraps
200g iceberg lettuce, shredded
 

Method

1. Heat the rice according to pack instructions.

2. Mix the ham hock with the sweetcorn and barbecue sauce. Warm the wraps in a microwave for 30 seconds.

3. Scatter the wraps with the lettuce and spoon half the rice down the centre of each one, then top with the pork mixture. Fold over the edges, then roll up and cut in half. Serve with salad or crisps on the side.
