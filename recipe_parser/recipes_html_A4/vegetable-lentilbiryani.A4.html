Vegetable & lentil biryani

  • Low Fat

Ingredients

600g tub fresh essential Waitrose Vegetable & Lentil Soup
1 tbsp tikka curry paste
150g frozen essential Waitrose Mixed Vegetables
100g essential Waitrose Basmati Rice
2 tbsp chopped coriander

Method

1. Place the soup in a large saucepan with the curry paste and mixed vegetables and bring to the boil. Stir inÂ the rice.

2. Cover and cook gently for about 15 minutes, stirring frequently to prevent sticking until the rice is tender and liquid has been absorbed. Stir in the coriander. Serve with naan bread if you wish.
