Baked potato with mackerel

Ingredients

2 baking potatoes
2 essential Waitrose Peppered Smoked Mackerel Fillets, skinned and boned
3 tbsp fat free natural yogurt
75g frozen sweetcorn
1 tbsp chopped chives

 

Method

1. Preheat the oven to 200Â°C, gas mark 6. Prick the potatoes with a fork and microwave on high for 5 minutes. Transfer to the oven for 30 minutes.

2. Meanwhile, place the mackerel and yogurt in a food processor and pulse to give a coarse paste.

3. Microwave the sweetcorn in a bowl covered with clingfilm until piping hot. Halve the potatoes, top with the sweetcorn and mackerel pÃ¢tÃ©, and sprinkle with chives.
